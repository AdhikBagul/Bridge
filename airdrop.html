The claim time limit has passed

Initially, 30,000,000,000 $DC (3% of the total supply) was to be rewarded to the early participants in the Dogechain system. The snapshot for eligibility included everyone that bridged Dogecoin from the launch of the mainnet (08.01.2022) until 24 hours before the token generation event (08.23.2022).
After the Great Burn, 80% of the initial total supply has been burned across all initial wallet allocations. Consequently the initial 30,000,000,000 $DC Early Shibes reward allocation was reduced to 6,000,000,000 $DC tokens.
In the first 2 airdrop iterations, 5,031,251,768 $DC tokens of the rewards have already been distributed.
Considering the completed airdrops, this leaves 968,748,232 $DC tokens to be distributed as rewards. Moreover, instead of 48 months, eligible users will be able to claim this amount in 6 months.
More precisely, each month, 161,458,038 $DC tokens will be distributed across all eligible users, proportionally to their Dogecoin holdings on Dogechain during the snapshot.
Be sure to claim your $DC tokens each month, othewise all furture Early Shibes airdrop will be forfeited.
