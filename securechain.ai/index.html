BENEFITS

Fast and Scalable blockchain

Securely scale your Dapps at lightspeed with the most advanced blockchain platform in the world. SecureChain’s Intelligent Consensus Engine (ICE) algorithm enables it to process thousands of transactions per second, making it highly scalable for real world applications.

Power of AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the future of technology. We are combining blockchain and AI technology to create a smarter blockchain platform especially in terms of security. This makes this blockchain platform more secure than traditional blockchains.

built-in security

Because of the rise in the crypto hacks and scams, our core focus is on creating chain which reduces the chances of scams and hacks by implementing AI based on-chain monitoring robots.

Utility focussed

Our on-chain products and other services will increase the utility and profitability which powers ecosystem growth and benefits to all the stakeholders, at the same time fulfilling our vision to keep the web3 a safer place.

Gasless transactions

SecureChain empowers users to use the platform without paying the gas fee. Although, paying gas prioritises the validation process, users can create tokens or NFT which does not need to pay any transaction fees and use it as if it is standalone coins.

Deflationary

SCAI coins are burned on every transaction, which reduces its supply and increases its scarcity, making it truly deflationary. This will increase the coin value over time.

Products and Services

Smart Contract Audit

Intensive smart contract audit performed to identify any security or logical vulnerabilities. This service generates major source of revenue in the ecosystem

KYC
Verification

KYC service is provided to blockchain businesses who want to increase trust in their community and at the same time remain anonymous to protect their privacy.

Due Diligence Service

This service is provided to investors who want to invest in any crypto project but want to verify its authenticity, reliability and credibility.

Enterprise Blockchain

This service lets other blockchain businesses launch their own mainnet blockchain to expand their ecosystem and increase the utility of thier businesses.

Freelance Network

SecureChain’s talent network who helps blockchain businesses to build and run their business. There will be a wide pool of talented professionals providing various services to blockchain businesses.

Escrow
service

SecureChain’s smart contract based escrow service provides a safe environment and increases trust between the buyer and seller to do business transactions in a decentralised world.

AI-based Forensics

This service lets victims trace down the hackers and helps them to recover their lost funds as much as possible. This service also can help regulators and law enforcement agencies.

Blacklist Database

This is a collection of all the scammy wallets, domains, etc. And this data will also be published in smart contracts. So, other Dapps can prevent interactions from such blacklisted wallets.
